Key Synergies

Artifact Flood: Omni-Cheese Pizza, Mouser Attack! tokens, Turtle Blimp token, Mutagen tokens — each one grows Donatello Way with Machines
Chrome Dome Lord: All artifact creatures (robots, vehicles) get +1/+0. Copy an Omni-Cheese or Jitte for {5}
Donatello Mechanic: Tap to put three +1/+1 counters on any artifact and animate it. Turn Omni-Cheese into a 3/3, Jitte into a 3/3 double-striker
Nobody Bounce: Return Omni-Cheese or Bespoke Bō to hand → replay for ETB value (draw card, bounce permanent) + grows Donatello
Double Strike Package: Hard-Won Jitte on any creature = double damage. On Mondo Gecko (hexproof) or a Donatello with counters = lethal
Mondo Gecko Engine: Draws + discards every end step. Filters to your bombs, fuels graveyard
Kitsune Finisher: 6/6 vigilance that steals a creature on ETB or combat damage. Swings the board
Free Splashes: Krang ({U/B}{U/B} = just {B}{B}), Squirrelanoids ({B}) — only need 1 Swamp + fixing

Why UR over BG?

The BG Disappear deck had a fatal flaw: weak payoffs. Insectoid Exterminator's scry 1 is not worth building around. The deck had 9 cards at CMC 4, splashed two colors, and the reward for all that complexity was... scry 1. It got run over by turn 4-5.

This UR build fixes everything:

• 11 two-drops vs the old deck's 5-6. You actually curve out and contest the board early.

• Real bombs: Mondo Gecko, Donatello Mechanic, Kitsune, Krang & Shredder, Cool but Rude, Chrome Dome — six rares/mythics that each win the game on their own.

• Artifact synergy has teeth: Unlike Disappear's scry, artifacts here create a snowball effect. Donatello grows, Chrome Dome pumps the team, Mechanic animates artifacts into threats, Nobody bounces for repeated ETBs.

• Minimal splash: Just 1 Swamp + Escape Tunnel + IB + 2 Omni-Cheese for Squirrelanoids and Krang hybrid. No greedy 4-color manabase.

• Real removal: Bot Bashing Time (6 damage exile), Cool but Rude (level 3 = 4 damage), Bespoke Bō (bounce), Kitsune (steal).
